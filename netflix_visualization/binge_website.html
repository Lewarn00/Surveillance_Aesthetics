The image above visualizes time spent on Netflix every day for a year. Each square represents one day and each column represents one week. The square in the bottom left corner represents the first day and the square in the top right corner represents the last. By clicking the hyperlinked numbers above each column of the visualization you can find more information about viewing patterns during that week (notably longest binges, which are times spent watching Netflix uninterrupted).

In the key above, the white square indicates less than 30 minutes spent on Netflix. The brightest red square indicates 30 - 60 minutes spent on Netflix. The next red square indicates 60 - 90 minutes spent on Netflix. The next red square indicates 90 - 200 minutes spent on Netflix. The next red square indicates 200 - 300 minutes spent on Netflix. Finally, the darkest red square indicates more than 300 minutes spent on Netflix in one day.
